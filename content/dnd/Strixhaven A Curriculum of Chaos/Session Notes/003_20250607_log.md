**Session Summary**

Previous Session: -  [[002_20250525_log|Session 2 Recap]]

Having been dismissed by [[Professor Serafina Onyx]] after the incident in the extra-dimensional mansion. [[Borun Erutan|Borun]], [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] and [[Dalaila Eversnow|Dalaila]] decided to go to [[Firejolt Cafe]] to drink coffee and revise, whereas [[Roleck Nevari|Roleck]] decided to go back to the dorm and revise in quiet.

As the students headed to the [[Firejolt cafe]] they were approached by another student who told them that a group of students had  found some strange looking frogs that they were going to race in one of the cubbies at [[Firejolt Cafe]].  In the cafe already were [[Omari Elderon|Omari]] and [[Zolon Orielson|Zolon]] .  

As the students approached the area just out of site of the barista station,  they saw that the students already there had moved the tables and chairs and chalked an outline of a track for the frogs to race on.  [[Bhedum Rampart Sooviij|Rampart]] and [[Tilana Kapule]]  were already ready to race two of the five frogs so [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]], [[Dalaila Eversnow|Dalaila]] and [[Omari Elderon|Omari]] all decided to race the remaining three frogs.  [[Borun Erutan|Borun]] and [[Zolon Orielson|Zolon]] decided to just be vocal during the race.

The race was broken down into three stages .  [[Borun Erutan|Borun]] made an early decision to cheer on [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] whilst it seemed as though [[Zolon Orielson|Zolon]] took a dislike to [[Bhedum Rampart Sooviij|Rampart]], at least judging by the insults that were thrown his way in an effort distract him from the race.  Both [[Borun Erutan|Borun]] and [[Zolon Orielson|Zolon]] were successful in their chosen strategies as[[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] took an early lead which he maintained for the entire race and [[Bhedum Rampart Sooviij|Rampart]] never really got off the mark.  The race ended with [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] winning and [[Bhedum Rampart Sooviij|Rampart]] losing in spectacular fashion.
