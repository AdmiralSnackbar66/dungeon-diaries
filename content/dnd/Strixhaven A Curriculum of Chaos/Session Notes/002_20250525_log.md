It's had been about two weeks since the incident in the Biblioplex and for the week immediately following the incident the party had become local celebrities even making the front page of the Strixhaven Star .

For the first week that was all anyone around campus could talk about and the story made the front page of the Strixhaven Star and those involved became local celebrities on campus.. but a week went by and soon the event was forgotten faculty and students started to move on and the local celebrity status didnt last.  


Everyone has started work and although some had little to no experience in the roles they applied for they've all been managing with passable success.  Everyone has met their co-workers and have, as of yet, had no conflict with any of them.  

[[Omari Elderon|Omari]] has only briefly met his co-worker [[Drazhomir Yarnask]] but [[Drazhomir Yarnask|Drazhomir]] hasn't shown up for his last shift and hasn't  been seen around campus in a while and although he's quiet he's hard to miss , what with him being a 7ft Minotaur ....  The last time he was seen was by [[Rook]] in the office he uses on the second floor of [[The Biblioplex]].

All of the party have been given the day to study for their upcoming exam on The Slaadi.  They all agree to meet in the Biblioplex to do some last minute revision.  [[Omari Elderon|Omari]] and [[Zolon Orielson|Zolon]] have decided to revise on their own in their dorm room instead of with the rest of the group.

The rest of the group ([[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]], [[Dalaila Eversnow|Dalaila]], [[Borun Erutan|Borun]] and [[Roleck Nevari|Roleck]]) find a table in the Biblioplex to start their revision but before they get a chance they are approached by [[Rook]] who says that [[Drazhomir Yarnask|Drazhomir]] has told him to come and ask the party for assistance. He goes on to explain that [[Drazhomir Yarnask|Drazhomir]] had found something unusual in [[The Biblioplex]] and would like them to assist with his discovery.  [[Rook]] takes the students to [[Drazhomir Yarnask|Drazhomir]]'s office on the second floor.

The students enter the office but there is no sign of [[Drazhomir Yarnask|Drazhomir]] .  They start to look around and [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] discovers an open book entitled *The Joy of Extra Dimensional Space* on the desk in the room.  He starts to investigate the book and notices handwritten notes within the book.  Most of the handwriting is unreadable with the exception of one word - "Scepter".  [[Elvis Parsley| Elvis]] speaks the word and some magical doors appear in the corner of the room.  As soon as they appear they start to fade and disappear and it is clear that they will disappear within a matter of minutes.  Without hesitation both [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] and [[Dalaila Eversnow|Dalaila]] walk through the door just before it vanishes.   [[Borun Erutan|Borun]] and [[Roleck Nevari|Roleck]] look on in disbelief.

[[Borun Erutan|Borun]] and [[Roleck Nevari|Roleck]] decide that they need to go and get help.  [[Roleck Nevari|Roleck]] grabs the book and they start to leave the office to go and get a member of the faculty.  Upon trying to leave with the book Roleck feels a shock coming from the book and it quickly becomes apparent that they can't leave the office with the book.  They decide to leave the book behind and place it back on the desk.  The book then flips open to the page it was on with the keyword that [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] spoke earlier.

They leave and go to look for a member of the faculty.

Meanwhile back in the extra dimensional space ... [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] and [[Dalaila Eversnow|Dalaila]] find themselves in the foyer of a huge mansion.  In the foyer is [[Drazhomir Yarnask]].  He is startled when [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] and [[Dalaila Eversnow|Dalaila]] step through the door. He explains to them that he has been trying to re-open the door for hours but with no luck and goes on to explain that he has the sufficient knowledge to open the door but only from the other side.  He shows the party members a statue of an imp that he has found and thinks the mansion they are in holds many magical treasures.  He needs to get back to Strixhaven as he wants to carry out experiments on the statue and also fears the faculty and [[The Biblioplex|Biblioplex]] staff have noticed his absence.
![[Lillianas_mansion.png|600]]

[[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] and [[Dalaila Eversnow|Dalaila]] start to explore the mansion by going to the room M9. Here they both hear giggling.and [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] feels something fly past his face.  Freaked out they both turn and run screaming all the way.

Meanwhile back at Strixhaven both [[Borun Erutan|Borun]] and [[Roleck Nevari|Roleck]] are unsuccessful finding any faculty members so they decide, reluctantly, to go back to the office and repeat the code word "Scepter" to see if that unlock the door..

In the mansion [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] and [[Dalaila Eversnow|Dalaila]] come running down the hallway just as [[Roleck Nevari|Roleck]] and [[Borun Erutan|Borun]]  walk through the door (M1 on the map).  [[Elvis Parsley|Elvis]] runs straight past them straight to location M2 on the map
