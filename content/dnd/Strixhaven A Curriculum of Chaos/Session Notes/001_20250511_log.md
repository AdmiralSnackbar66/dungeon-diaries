The player characters  all arrive at Strixhaven University and, as they are waiting for the orientation to start they fall asleep and seem to share the same dream, although as they don't know each other at this stage they have no recollection of seeing each other in the dream.  

They dream of the aftermath of a huge battle, death and destruction all around and the remains of a building in the centre of a University campus still ablaze. One cloaked figure appearing triumphant and victorious. Then another student  rises to their feet, looks towards the players . All of sudde their senses return as they hear the student shout REMEMBER whilst pointing a wand at them....... with that they awaken in the waiting area as [[Rosimyffenbip Rosie Wuzzfedlims]] comes to fetch them for the beginning of their tour.

At this point the player characters all introduce themselves to each other. 

They introduce themselves to [[Rosimyffenbip Rosie Wuzzfedlims|Rosie]] and each other as she takes them to meet the Year 1 counsellor - [[Professor Mavinda Sharpbeak]] 

She gives them a whirlwind tour of the [[ttrpgs/Campaigns/Strixhaven/Locations/The Biblioplex]] and shows them key locations they will need to know whilst studying as first years at Strixhaven.  After the tour , its time for the first item on their itinerary - a scavenger hunt.

The scavenger hunt consists of 5 clues as follows : - 

![[ScavengerHunt.png]]

[[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Elvis Parsley]] seemed to take the lead initially saying that he felt the first clue related to the Snarl in the Hall of Oracles within the Biblioplex, it was [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Omari Elderon]] investigative skills that pushed him to approach the Snarl and reach into the basing beneath the Snarl. Upon doing so he felt weightless and lost all sense of time (actually it was only a matter of seconds) as he withdrew his hand from the Snarls basin he held a potion of healing in his hand.  Seeing this [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Borun Erutan]] tried the same and was also rewarded with a healing potion.  [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Elvis Parsley]] then tried the same but with no success.

Clue 2 next and the party deduced that this must be referring to the student and alumni art gallery. As they entered the gallery and quickly scanned the surroundings they were draw towards a portrait of several older alumni wearing Prismari college robes . Upon further investigation they noticed a plaque beneath the portrait with a hidden lever. Upon activating the lever the plaque flipped and a draw revealed a Strixhaven pennant from Witherbloom.  Upon waving the pennant the  Witherbloom logo started to glitter and then emitted a bright light in a 10 feet radius and a dim light for a further 10 feet

On to clue 3 and and some free food as the group deduce this clue must be referring to the Biblioplex cafe. Dragonbreatnh peppers and salami on a bagel for those that wanted it along with a plain coffee or tea for those that wanted it.

Clue 4 sent the party towards the book gardens and towards the shrubbery. One particular shrubs leaves seemed to be glistening so [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Borun Erutan|Borun]] took it upon himself to take the clue literally and pat the leaves of the shrub. This caused the leaves to part and to reveal a Cuddly Strixhaven mascot in the colours of Silverquill.

The fifth and final clue now and the party head towards the ampithater.  [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Elvis Parsley]] took to the stage and started to sing.. This caused 6 bright stage floodlights to come on. When [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Roleck Nevari|Roleck]] investigated the floodlights and revealed a recess which contained a hooded lantern.

After successful job applications and signing up for extra curriculars the party made their way back to [[ttrpgs/Campaigns/Strixhaven/Locations/The Biblioplex]] .  Upon entering  a young elf came running towards them from shelving area to the west of [[ttrpgs/Campaigns/Strixhaven/Locations/The Biblioplex]] screaming about two of the trunks used by the Live-Action Roleplaying Guild, with the faculty mages taking care of the panicked students , the party stepped up to deal with the threat.  The two trunks were actually mimics !!

The battle went back and forward.  During the battle [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Zolon Orielson]] went down on more than one occasion.  The first instance was caused by an erratic Chromatic Orb being cast by [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Roleck Nevari]] that hit the mimic and bounced off and hit [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Zolon Orielson|Zolon]] wiping him out in one hit. Fortunately for [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Zolon Orielson|Zolon]] , [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Dalaila Eversnow]]'s  battle medic skills and [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Omari Elderon]]'s use of a healing potion managed to ensure he didn't die.

When all of the dust settled  the mimics were defeated.  [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Elvis Parsley]] examined the remains of the chests and noticed there were traces of an oily black substance which he bottled in the empty healing potion bottle for further examination.

[[Professor Mavinda Sharpbeak]] appeared to congratulate the party on their valiant efforts and rewarded each party member with 1 platinum and assured the party that this sort of thing hardly ever happens at Strixhaven so they need not worry.
