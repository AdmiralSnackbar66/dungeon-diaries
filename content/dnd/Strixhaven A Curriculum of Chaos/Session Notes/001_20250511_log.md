The player characters  all arrive at Strixhaven University and, as they are waiting for the orientation to start they fall asleep and seem to share the same dream, although as they don't know each other at this stage they have no recollection of seeing each other in the dream.  

They dream of the aftermath of a huge battle, death and destruction all around and the remains of a building in the centre of a University campus still ablaze. One cloaked figure appearing triumphant and victorious. Then another student  rises to their feet, looks towards the players . All of sudde their senses return as they hear the student shout REMEMBER whilst pointing a wand at them....... with that they awaken in the waiting area as Rosimyffenbip "Rosie" Wuzfeddlims comes to fetch them for the beginning of their tour.

At this point the player characters all introduce themselves to each other. 

They introduce themselves to [[Rosimyffenbip "Rosie" Wuzzfedlims|Rosie]] and each other as she takes them to meet the Year 1 counsellor - [[Professor Mavinda Sharpbeak]] 

She gives them a whirlwind tour of the [[The Biblioplex]] and shows them key locations they will need to know whilst studying as first years at Strixhaven.  After the tour , its time for the first item on their itinerary - a scavenger hunt.
