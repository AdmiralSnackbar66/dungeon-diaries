Originally a Quandrix professor of theory, this middle-aged vedalken now teaches a series of general studies courses that teaches students bout the basics of the different casting schools of magic. While many of the Quandrix faculty focuses on advanced theory, Orvlo has always been drawn to the basics of magical composition, particularly the fine details that make the different magical schools unique from one another. Orvlo typically teaches from the Biblioplex, where he spends most of his time. When not teaching, he works as a volunteer librarian, using his skills to detect any spells students may have attempted to place on the tomes or scrolls and subsequently dispelling them of any such enchantments. He appreciates hard work and earnest study, and frowns on students who attempt jokes on him or who try to ingratiate themselves with him.
