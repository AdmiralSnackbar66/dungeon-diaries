# Crawling
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 364* 

While you're crawling, each foot of movement costs 1 extra foot (2 extra feet in [difficult terrain](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/difficult-terrain-xphb.md)).
