# Searing Smite
*1st-level, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Bonus Action
- **Range:** Self
- **Components:** V
- **Duration:** 1 minute

As you hit the target, it takes an extra `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6`) Fire damage from the attack. At the start of each of its turns until the spell ends, the target takes `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d6`) Fire damage and then makes a Constitution saving throw. On a failed save, the spell continues. On a successful save, the spell ends.

**Using a Higher-Level Spell Slot.** All the damage increases by `dice:1d6|noform|noparens|avg|text(1d6)` for each spell slot level above 1.
