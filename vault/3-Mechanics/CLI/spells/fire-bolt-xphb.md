# Fire Bolt
*cantrip, Evocation*  


- **Casting time:** 1 Action
- **Range:** 120 feet
- **Components:** V, S
- **Duration:** Instantaneous

You hurl a mote of fire at a creature or an object within range. Make a ranged spell attack against the target. On a hit, the target takes `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d10`) Fire damage. A flammable object hit by this spell starts [burning](/3-Mechanics/CLI/traps-hazards/burning-xphb.md) if it isn't being worn or carried.

**Cantrip Upgrade.** The damage increases by `dice:1d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`1d10`) when you reach levels 5 (`dice:2d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`2d10`)), 11 (`dice:3d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`3d10`)), and 17 (`dice:4d10|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d10`)).
