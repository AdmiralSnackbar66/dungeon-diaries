>[!readaloud] 
>Its the day before your exam and  you are wanting to get some last minute reading in on the Slaadi so you've made your way to the Biblioplex.. 
>
>When you arrive you find a table to go over the notes you've taken in class and you are approached by [[Rook]].  They approach Omari and say
>>[!cite] Rook
>>Greetings Master [[ttrpgs/Player Characters/Omari Elderon|Omari]]. I have a message for you from Master [[04-school-is-in-session#Drazhomir Yarnask|Drazhomir]].  He has found something odd in the great [[Biblioplex]] and requires your assistance  ...

>[! hint]
> If the students don't agree then continue with the [[7. The Joy of Extradimensional Space#Studying for the exam|Studying for the exam]].  If they do agree then continue to read.

>[!readaloud] 
>[[Rook]] leads you all to one of the advanced student offices where [[Drazhomir Yarnask| Drazhomir]] was last seen.  
>In the office there is a desk with a handful of books but no sign of  [[Drazhomir Yarnask|Drazhomir]].  One book in particular, catches your attention.  *The Joy of Extradimensional Spaces*  as it 's the only open book on the desk..  On the open page of the book seem to be handwritten notes.

>[! hint]
> A character who succeeds on a DC 13 Intelligence ([[3-Mechanics/CLI/skills#Arcana|Arcana]]) check can decipher the shorthand and find the command word "scepter".  If a player speaks the command word a shimmering translucent doors appear in the middle of the room.  The doors begin to slowly fade and it is apparent that the doors will disappear entirely in a matter of minutes.

>[!readaloud] 
>You emerge in the foyer of a huge mansion. The doors you have just come through just appear to be normal double doors.
>
>The ceiling arches to fifteen feet overhead and long hallways stretch out on either side of the foyer.  Standing in the middle of this area is [[Drazhomir Yarnask]]
>He is startled when you walk through the door.

>[!cite] Drazhomir Yarnask
>Thank goodness I've been trying to get this door open for at least an hour....
>I have a feeling that this location is a veritable treasure trove of  information and there is no telling what wonders it holds.

>[!readaloud] 
>Drazhomir shows you an intricately carved statue of an imp he has found and wants to take it back to Strixhaven for further study.

>[!cite] Drazhomir
>I would dearly like to continue to explore this mansion s mysteries myself but fear that my absence from Strixhaven has been noticed.   Thanks to the research I've done I now have the skills to keep the portal open from the other side and if you would continue to explore this mansion for me I would be forever grateful..
>

>[!hint]  
>If the players agree , then read on, other wise Drazhomir and the players step through the portal back to Strixhaven and then read [[7. The Joy of Extradimensional Space#^4b3fdb|Studying for the exam]]

>[!readaloud]
>[[Drazhomir Yarnask|Drazhomir]] steps through the portal and as the doors close you hear him shriek as the imp figurine he is holding reveals its true nature.  You can't help him until you find a way to open the portal.

>[!info]
>The imp figurine in Matreous's possession (see area M1) appears to be a very detailed carving of an imp made from onyx. It's not a statue, however, but a real [imp](/3-Mechanics/CLI/bestiary/fiend/imp-xmm.md) that Fistandia summoned and transformed into a statuette. Removing the figurine from the mansion breaks the enchantment and releases the imp.
